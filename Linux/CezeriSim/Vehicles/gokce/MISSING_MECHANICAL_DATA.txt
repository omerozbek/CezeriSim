 2. CENTER OF GRAVITY POSITION                                    PRIORITY: HIGH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Why: cm_alpha (pitch stability, currently -0.7 = assumed 15% static margin) is
 set by CG position relative to the wing's neutral point. Wrong CG → wrong trim
 AoA, wrong cruise pitch attitude, wrong elevator trim — visible in every
 fixed-wing log segment. Also confirms hover trim (CG vs lift-motor rectangle
 center).

 How to measure: balance the flight-ready vehicle (battery in) on a rod or two
 scales; measure distance from the wing root LEADING EDGE, positive aft.

 FILL IN:
   CG aft of wing root leading edge (mm)          = ______
   Wing root chord (mm)                           = ______   (to express CG in %MAC)
   CG lateral offset from centerline, if any (mm) = ______
   CG below/above wing chord plane (mm, +down)    = ______
   CG position vs center of the 4 lift motors:
       forward/aft offset (mm, +aft)              = ______
