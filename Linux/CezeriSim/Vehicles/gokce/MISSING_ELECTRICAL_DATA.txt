 3. MOTOR RESPONSE TIME (SPIN-UP LAG)                           PRIORITY: MEDIUM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Why: motor_time_constant_s 0.05 is a generic value. It shapes how the plant
 responds to every rate-controller correction — affects hover PID texture and
 the attitude-oscillation signature you compare in logs.

 How to measure: on the thrust stand, command a throttle step (e.g. 30%→60%)
 and record thrust or RPM vs time; the time constant is time to reach 63% of
 the change. A stand log file at ≥100 Hz is ideal — attach it if you have one.

 FILL IN:
   Lift motor (P60):   step ____% → ____%,  time to 63% = ______ ms
   Pusher (AT5220):    step ____% → ____%,  time to 63% = ______ ms
   (or attach the stand's time-series log)

 4. BATTERY PACK & POWER SYSTEM                                    PRIORITY: LOW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Why: the UE plant does not consume battery data YET — thrust is voltage-
 independent in the sim. On the real vehicle, pack sag over a 5-minute mission
 reduces available thrust several percent (visible as hover throttle creep in
 logs). This data enables a future voltage-sag model and realistic battery
 telemetry (SIM_BATT_* in ArduPilot), and the voltage column in sections 1–2
 is only interpretable with pack specs.
